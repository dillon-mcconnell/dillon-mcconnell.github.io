About

I'm an analyst focused on investment funds and financial data, with a background in management science. Day to day that means querying equity data, untangling fund methodology, and writing explanations a client can actually follow.

Outside the spreadsheet I build small web apps to scratch my own itches — interactive tools, data visualizations, and the occasional weekend project. I care about clarity: in the analysis, in the writing, and in the interface.
